Frequently Asked Questions

Is CookedAI free to use?

CookedAI offers both free and premium features. Check the app for the latest details on available plans.

How do I cancel a subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings > your name > Subscriptions on your device to manage or cancel your subscription.

The app is crashing or not loading properly.

Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the issue persists, make sure you're running the latest version from the App Store. If the problem continues, email us with details about your device and iOS version.

How do I request my data be deleted?

Send an email to cookedaisupport@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and we will process your request within 30 days.
